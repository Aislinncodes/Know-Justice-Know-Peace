What We Do
Know Justice Know Peace is a platform focused on magnifying the voices speaking on racial injustice and anti-racism. KJKP houses a directory for organizations that specialize in this work. We also offer resources such as books, articles, etc to educate readers on issues dealing with race. Individuals have the opportunity to volunteer and/or donate to their organization of choice. We realize a lot of people may not know all the work being done to better the world. KJKP is here to showcase that work.
